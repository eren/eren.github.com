About

I have been interested in software and Internet technologies since I was 13. In high school, I studied English language and later applied to English Language Teaching department in university. After one year, my ambition for programming led me to study Computer Science as a second major.

I love learning new technologies and I am interested programming languages, operating systems, networking, embedded systems, system administration and dev-ops. I try to look at the big picture when developing a project with questions: how will it be deployed, scaled, tested, and how can we make it better?

I know teaching methodologies, classroom management, assessment, evaluation, and the actual classroom environment based on my experiences in English Language Teaching department. I want to blend this experience with Computer Science skills to help people learn better.
